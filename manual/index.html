setup

in order to play this game, you need charts.

about charts

there are two supported formats: tja and osu.

osu charts are the easiest to get, as you simply download them from the official website or a mirror.

tja charts are scattered throughout the internet. you'll have to seek them out yourself.

where to put charts

put the charts in .domtaiko/songs/.

offset adjustment

how offset works

basically, positive values will make the notes appear later, and negative values will make the notes appear later.

global offset

global offset can be adjusted from the settings menu.

local offset

during gameplay, you can press # on your keypad to get the following to appear in the corner:

this will display the local offset for the chart, the global offset for the entire game, and the current average hit error for the entire current attempt.

while holding #, you can also press the directional keys to adjust the local offset, or press 1 on your keypad to reset the local offset back to zero.

custom hitsounds

place custom hitsounds in the directory .domtaiko/custom/.

the filenames are don.ogg and kat.ogg, with each filename representing their respective sound effect.

game modifiers

this section will describe the game modifiers.

autoplay

this will have your device play the chart to the best of its ability.

enable or disable by pressing 1 on your keypad on the song select screen.

audio playback rate modify

this will change the playback rate of the audio and chart.

modify this on the song select screen.

scroll speed modify

this will change the scroll speed, that is the speed that notes move from right to left.

modify this on the song select screen.

metronome

this will play a metronome.

this can be enabled or disabled by going to settings > sound settings > metronome

timing window adjust

this will adjust the timing window. the accompanying labels are W for wide, T for tight, and C for custom.

this can be modified by going to settings > game settings > hit windows

hide notes

this will make the notes not visible. show off your memorization skills with this game modifier.

this can be enabled or disabled by going to settings > visual settings > hide notes
