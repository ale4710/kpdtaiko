setup

in order to play this game, you need charts.

about charts

there are two supported formats: tja and osu.

osu charts are the easiest to get, as you simply download them from the official website or a mirror.

tja charts are scattered throughout the internet. you'll have to seek them out yourself.

important: for tja charts, diverge notes are NOT supported.

where to put charts

put the charts in .domtaiko/songs/.

if you have a file in zip or osk format, that means that you have a song archive, and it should be put in .domtaiko/import/ instead. please see the song import tool for more details.

song import tool

the song import tool is a tools that can automatically extract song archives and import them into the song library.

how to use

  1. place song archives in the import folder of the game folder. it can extract zip archives only. (note: osz files are also zips)
  2. open the game, and select the "Import" option on the title screen.
  3. follow the on screen instructions (it should be pretty obvious from here on out)
  4. you are done

important notes

  • i wrote this really quickly, so it probably will break pretty easily.
  • this will only extract the required files needed for gameplay.
  • the archives won't be removed after they are imported. please delete them yourself once you confirm that they're imported correctly.

hit windows

there are settings that allow you to modify the hit windows.

hit window sizes

the following is a table that lists the allowed deviation from the target time.

so 10ms would mean that the window is 20ms wide. 10ms too early and 10ms too late.

size good okay miss
normal 35ms 80ms 95ms
wide 46ms 104ms 124ms
tight 24ms 56ms 67ms

hit window modification

you are allowed to create your own hit windows.

the settings are located at settings > game settings > hit windows.

the number you put in will be the allowed deviation from the target time.

about miss hit window

the miss hit window is a kind of "spam" protection.

if you were to spam "don" in a chart full of dons, it is likely that you will land within the miss hit window.

if you play normally, however, it's unlikely (but still possible) that you'll hit the miss hit window.

offset adjustment

how offset works

basically, positive values will make the notes appear later, and negative values will make the notes appear later.

global offset

global offset can be adjusted from the settings menu.

local offset

during gameplay, you can press and hold # on your keypad to get the following to appear in the corner:

this will display the local offset for the chart, the global offset for the entire game, and the current average hit error for the entire current attempt.

while holding #, you can also press the directional keys to adjust the local offset, or press 1 on your keypad to reset the local offset back to zero.

about screenshots

on most devices you can press * and # to take a screenshot. this means that you can take a screenshot of the results screen. however, this obviously overlaps with the keybind for the offset display.

if you don't want the offset display to show up while taking a screenshot, you can press * first before pressing #. Pressing * first will prevent the offset display from showing up.

game modifiers

this section will describe the game modifiers.

autoplay

this will have your device play the chart to the best of its ability.

enable or disable by pressing 1 on your keypad on the song select screen.

audio playback rate modify

this will change the playback rate of the audio and chart.

modify this on the song select screen.

scroll speed modify

this will change the scroll speed, that is the speed that notes move from right to left.

modify this on the song select screen.

metronome

this will play a metronome.

this can be enabled or disabled by going to settings > sound settings > metronome

risky

this will limit the amount of misses you can have.

once you hit the miss limit, gameplay is stopped.

this can be modified by going to settings > game settings > risky

timing window adjust

this will adjust the timing window. the accompanying labels are W for wide, T for tight, and C for custom.

this can be modified by going to settings > game settings > hit windows

hide notes

this will make the notes not visible. show off your memorization skills with this game modifier.

this can be enabled or disabled by going to settings > visual settings > hide notes
